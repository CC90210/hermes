# Why "Hermes"

In Greek mythology, Hermes was the god of commerce, merchants, messengers, and travelers. He moved between worlds carrying messages, facilitating trade between buyers and sellers.

Your commerce agent does exactly this: it moves between your email inbox and your POS system, carrying purchase orders in and invoices back out. It's the messenger that handles the trade.

Hermes is built by OASIS AI Solutions for wholesale businesses that need their back-office to run itself.

**Pronunciation:** HER-meez
**Role:** Your personal commerce operations agent
**First name to speak aloud:** "Hey Hermes, what's the status?"
